Visualization of FIFA 18 statistics.
Being one of the most popular games, FIFA gamers still face some problems when they want to get a player for their team with specific attributes. By analyzing the dataset, we would to make it easier to visualize the attributes of the players. In addition, we would also show the inter-relationship between the players. The creaters of this project are Shikhat Karkee (s.karkee@jacobs-university.de) and Sabin Bhandari (sa.bhandari@jacobs-university.de).
