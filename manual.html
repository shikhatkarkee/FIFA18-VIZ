Visualization of FIFA 18 statistics.
Being one of the most popular games, FIFA gamers still face some problems when they want to get a player for their team with specific attributes. By analyzing the dataset, we would to make it easier to visualize the attributes of the players. In addition, we would also show the inter-relationship between the players. The creaters of this project are Shikhat Karkee (s.karkee@jacobs-university.de) and Sabin Bhandari (sa.bhandari@jacobs-university.de).

User Manual

  • Different clickable menus are available on the top of our webpage.
  • Please scroll down on the homepage to see the visualization.
  • Each topic can be clicked on to open the required visualization. Arrows are also available to toggle between different visualization.
  • Filters can be applied using slider buttons or the drop down list available at the right side of the visualization window.
  • Multiple options are available at the bottom of the visualization. You can download it, view it in full screen and toggle.
  • You can hover over different parts of the visualization which will pop up a small textbox giving you extra information.
  • You can also click on different parts of the visualization to view it in detail.
